Practical, editable templates for B2B buyers planning stainless steel cookware, tri-ply cookware, OEM/ODM, and private label sourcing projects.

Use these tools to define requirements, prepare supplier conversations, compare proposals, review samples, and organize compliance questions. The templates are intended for importers, wholesale distributors, private label brands, retail buying teams, HoReCa suppliers, sourcing agents, and product managers.

## Recommended Workflow

1. Start with the **Project Brief** for a new brand, collection, or packaging program.
2. Define the product construction using the **Tri-ply Specification Template** when planning stainless steel clad projects.
3. Convert the project requirements into a structured **RFQ** and distribute it to shortlisted suppliers.
4. Compare supplier quotes, certifications, and capabilities using the **Evaluation Scorecard**.
5. Inspect first prototypes and golden samples using the **Sample Inspection Checklist**.
6. Audit laboratory test reports and food-contact compliance using the **FDA / LFGB Certification Checklist** before signing the purchase order.

You can view or download the original Markdown files from the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/goldensea-kitchenware/cookware-sourcing-toolkit).

## Important Notice

These templates are sourcing aids, not legal, regulatory, testing, or quality assurance advice. Requirements vary by destination market, product design, materials, food-contact surfaces, packaging claims, and importer responsibilities. Confirm applicable requirements with qualified laboratories, compliance consultants, customs brokers, or legal advisors before production or shipment.
