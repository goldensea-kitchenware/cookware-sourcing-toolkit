## 1. Tri-ply Structure

Tri-ply cookware usually uses a bonded structure with a food-contact stainless steel interior, an aluminum heat-conducting core, and a magnetic stainless steel exterior for induction compatibility. Confirm the exact structure with the supplier and testing partner.
