# Cookware Sourcing Toolkit

Practical Markdown templates for B2B buyers preparing stainless steel cookware, tri-ply cookware, OEM/ODM cookware, and private label cookware sourcing projects.

This toolkit is designed for importers, wholesale distributors, private label brands, retail buying teams, HoReCa suppliers, sourcing agents, and product managers who need to turn a cookware idea into a clear supplier conversation.

Created by [Goldensea / GS Kitchenware](https://www.gskitchenware.com/), a China-based OEM/ODM cookware manufacturer focused on stainless steel and tri-ply cookware projects.

## What This Repository Includes

| File | Use it when you need to |
| --- | --- |
| `rfq-checklist.md` | Prepare a complete cookware RFQ before contacting suppliers. |
| `supplier-evaluation-scorecard.md` | Compare cookware suppliers with a structured scorecard. |
| `cookware-sample-inspection-checklist.md` | Review samples before confirming a bulk order. |
| `tri-ply-cookware-spec-template.md` | Define tri-ply cookware specifications for supplier discussion. |
| `certification-checklist-fda-lfgb.md` | Prepare compliance questions and document checks for FDA/LFGB-related projects. |
| `private-label-cookware-project-brief.md` | Brief a supplier on a private label cookware program. |

## How to Use These Templates

1. Copy the relevant Markdown file into your internal sourcing workspace.
2. Replace the placeholder fields with your project details.
3. Share the completed version with shortlisted suppliers.
4. Ask suppliers to respond directly against the checklist instead of sending only a general catalog.
5. Keep supplier answers, sample findings, and risk notes in the same document for decision review.

## Recommended Workflow

For a new cookware sourcing project, use the documents in this order:

1. Start with `private-label-cookware-project-brief.md` if the project involves a new brand, collection, or packaging system.
2. Use `tri-ply-cookware-spec-template.md` if the product is tri-ply stainless steel cookware.
3. Convert the project requirements into an RFQ with `rfq-checklist.md`.
4. Compare supplier replies with `supplier-evaluation-scorecard.md`.
5. Review first samples with `cookware-sample-inspection-checklist.md`.
6. Use `certification-checklist-fda-lfgb.md` before finalizing market, testing, and documentation expectations.

## Important Notes

- These templates are sourcing aids, not legal, regulatory, or quality assurance advice.
- Compliance requirements vary by market, product design, material, food-contact surface, and importer responsibility.
- Buyers should confirm applicable regulations with qualified testing labs, compliance consultants, customs brokers, or legal advisors before shipment.
- Avoid assuming that a supplier's previous report automatically applies to a new product, new coating, new handle material, new packaging claim, or new destination market.
